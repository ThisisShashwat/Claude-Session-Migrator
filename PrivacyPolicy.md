# Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Claude Session Migrator & Account Manager is designed to help users manage multiple Claude accounts and continue conversations when usage limits are reached.

## Information We Access

The extension may access:

* Claude conversation content necessary for chat migration.
* Authentication cookies required for switching between user-added accounts.
* Account usage information and refresh times.
* Extension settings stored locally.

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally in the user's browser. The extension does not sell, share, or transmit personal information to third parties.

## External Servers

Claude Session Migrator & Account Manager does not operate external servers for collecting user data. Conversation data is used solely to enable account switching and conversation continuation.

## Third-Party Services

The extension interacts only with Claude's website as part of its intended functionality.

## Your Control

Users may remove accounts, clear extension data, or uninstall the extension at any time.

## Contact

For questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact the developer through the Chrome Web Store listing.
